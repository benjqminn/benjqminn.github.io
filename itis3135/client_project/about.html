Meet Jaylee

Jaylee Vasquez is the founder of BuiltBunny Personal Training - a certified personal trainer on a mission to help you level up physically and mentally. She specializes in building glutes, confidence, and routines that stick. Jaylee is also recently certified in teaching group fitness classes and is happy to announce her new group fitness programs!

Philosophy

“Fitness should be empowering, not intimidating.”

BuiltBunny is a brand made to embrace strength and softness. Jaylee creates a safe, uplifting environment where your growth is the priority.

More About Jaylee

In addition to running BuiltBunny, Jaylee is currently attending the University of North Carolina at Charlotte where she is pursuing a degree in Elementary Education.
